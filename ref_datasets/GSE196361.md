# GSE196361
## preprocessing
- GSE196361 is a preprocessed dataset. In the original dataset cluster 11 and 21 are annotated as macrophages. We use marker gene expression to confirm the classification.<br>
![marker expression](images/GSE196361/Macrophage_markers_cluster.png)
- Macrophages are extracted and reclustered. This gives 17 clusters with res = 0.5.
![clusters](images/GSE196361/clusters.png)
